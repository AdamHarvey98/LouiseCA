Sean Cronin better known as Invader Slim is an Irish Hip-Hop artist hailing from Wicklow. Slim began releasing music at the start of 2018 and since then has brought his doom-shrouded rap to the scene.

He has released a plethora of songs and videos in the short time he has been in the scene and has strived to experiment and push the boundaries of what Irish Hip Hop is. Succesfully racking up plays and views online with various different outlets such as Vice and District Magazine showing their support.

His enticing flows and lyrics are at their best on songs such as Mothership and No Hook where Slim does what he does best, making great music. Links to these songs, videos and his social links can be found on this site including a link to his new blog Midas Mag.
